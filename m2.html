M2 - Validation 1 (idea)

  • Identifying the problem

    • Browsing the web looking for fresh new startups in Romania doesn’t end with a satisfactory result.

    From an investor’s point of view, there is no way you can find an opportunity for investment in an easy manner. Also, even if you may discover an article or two presenting a startup on a website, getting in touch with company owners can be complicated if they do not provide contact details.

    On the other hand, from an entrepreneurial point of view, presenting a new business can be a tough thing at first, when financial resources are limited and you can’t afford marketing campaigns. With no public display of your idea, you will never get an investment to get you on track.

  • Finding the solution

    • Naturally, a way to solve the above problem is to bring both investors and entrepreneurs together in the same place. The solution translates into a platform that both sides will have access to, tailored to the needs of each.

    Therefore, an entrepreneur is encouraged to present his idea of business, along with essential details on the financial status of his startup. He can present the financial developments over a recent period of time, if he makes profit or just covers his expenses, and also if he needs other kind of resources, not necessarily money.

    The more companies are registered, the higher the offer will be for investors, increasing the chances of investing in a startup that suits their desires. Moreover, all the investors can leave feedback to any company or help with various tips, which is an opportunity for companies to evolve in the right direction.

  • Plan for customer discovery

    • Before we start having conversations with potential customers, we will define the idea of the problem we believe we are solving with our product. Then, we try to find our customer segment, the people that are directly affected by the problem. Once we have a basic outline of the problem and our solution, we’re ready to test our hypothesis. With all the feedback that we gather from potential customers, we will figure out if our original hypothesis about the problem and our solution were correct.
