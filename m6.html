M6 - Market Research

  • Determine the volume of your target market (including the different sub-markets)
    •

    Our target market is defined by two customer segments: investors and entrepreneurs. Both segments will use different services provided by the platform, depending on their needs.

    In terms of volume, we found in our research some interesting statistics:
  • Investors - unfortunately, the number of Romanian investors has fallen lately because of the lack of financial knowledge and market uncertainty. Most of SMEs are looking for foreign investments, and their number actually increased this year by 17,6%.
  • Entrepreneurs - according to this article, Romania ranks second, after the UK, in terms of increasing the number of SMEs in 2013-2017. During this period of time, the total number of newly established businesses reached 499,419.
